About This Event

Time: 4 Hours

Every Sunday*, Katrina hosts beginner-friendly pottery classes at Elizabeth Street in the heart of Brisbane City. Join other pottery enthusiasts and make new friends in the art world of pottery! Although lessons are specifically catered towards those new at pottery, experienced potters are still welcome to join in. All supplies will be provided (listed down below), so just bring yourself and your friends.

Supplies Include:

  • Pottery wheels
  • Studio tools
  • Under-glazes for decorating
  • Clay

*May be canceled on some Sundays, please check Katrina's profile for updates.
